Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Winmoon Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Nila mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile Number (OTP Authentication)
  • Name
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Profile Photo
  • City / Location (with your permission)
  • Device Information
  • Firebase User ID
  • Wallet & Coin Balance
  • Transaction History
  • Voice & Video Call Records (service-related information only)
  • Chat Messages
  • Gift Transactions

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Verify your identity using OTP.
  • Provide voice and video calling services.
  • Enable messaging between users.
  • Process wallet transactions and virtual gifts.
  • Improve app performance and security.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse.

3. Permissions Used

  • Camera – Required for video calls and profile photos.
  • Microphone – Required for voice and video calls.
  • Location – Used only with your permission to improve user experience.
  • Storage / Photos – Used to upload profile images.
  • Notifications – Used for messages, calls, gifts, and important updates.

4. Wallet & Payments

Nila provides wallet and virtual coin services. Payments are processed using secure payment providers. We do not store your debit card, credit card, or banking credentials.

5. Firebase Services

Our application uses Firebase services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)

These services help securely authenticate users, store application data, and deliver notifications.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your information may only be shared:

  • When required by law.
  • To provide services through trusted technology partners.
  • To investigate fraud or security issues.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Nila is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly within the app by navigating to:

Account → Delete Account

If you cannot access your account, you may contact our support team to request deletion.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
